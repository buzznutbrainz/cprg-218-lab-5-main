UNO Skip Card

Rules:

The Skip card has the following effects in the game:

  • Skip the next player: When played, the next player in the turn order is skipped and loses their turn.
  • Two-player game: In a two-player game, playing a Skip card makes you immediately take another turn, effectively causing the other player to lose their next turn.
  • Strategy use: Can be used strategically to prevent the next player from playing, especially if they have only one card left.
