Uno Draw Cards

Rules:

The Draw 2 and Draw 4 cards have the following effects in the game:

  • Draw 2: The next player must draw two cards and lose their turn.
  • Draw 4: The next player must draw four cards and lose their turn. Additionally, the player who plays this card can choose the next color to be matched.
  • Challenge: In some variations, if the next player thinks the Draw 4 was played illegally (the player had a matching card), they can challenge it. If the challenge is successful, the original player draws four instead; otherwise, the challenger draws six.
